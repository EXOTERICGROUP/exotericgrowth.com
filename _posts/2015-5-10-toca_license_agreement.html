      FIXED FEE means
      THIS AGREEMENT IS FOR A FIXED FEE - WHY? - ITS SIMPLE, STRAIGHT FORWARD and BEST FOR BOTH PARTIES! Probably the simplest way to structure license fees (otherwise referred to as royalties) is through a fixed fee payment. Under such a model, the licensee pays a set amount determined at the time the agreement is signed. A benefit of the fixed fee to the licensor is the certainty of revenue either up front or at predetermined installment dates. From the perspectives of both licensor and licensee, there is the advantage of not having to track royalty payment.
